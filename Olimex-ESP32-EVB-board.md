
**Ethernet (RMII interface):**

| Signal | GPIO |
|--------|------|
| MDC | GPIO23 |
| MDIO | GPIO18 |

PHY is LAN8720 at address 0. Power GPIO is not used.

**SD Card (MMC interface):**

| Signal | GPIO |
|--------|------|
| CLK/SCLK | GPI14 |
| CMD/DI | GPIO15 |
| DAT0/DO | GPIO2 |

CD and WP signal are not used.
